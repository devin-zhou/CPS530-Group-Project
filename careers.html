A Career at Pizza Time

Our Values

Here at Pizza Time, we're hard workers. We take pride in the food we put on the table and strive to make the best pizza our customers have ever had. As a long standing downtown pizza parlour, we've become a staple to the communities we serve, with many regular customers. Our customer service is one of our strengths, and employees are expected to uphold this long standing relationship. Pizza Time is a brand built on inclusivity, respect, and care.

Requirements:

  • No prior experience required
  • At least 16 years old
  • Customer service skills
  • Availible during store hours
  • Able to do manual labour (lifting supplies)
  • Able to work in a team
  • Able to work in a hot environment
  • A driver's liscence (If you are applying for delivery)
